# Platelets

## What it measures
The platelet count measures the number of platelets in the blood. Platelets are small cell fragments that help blood clot and stop bleeding.

## Why a value may be low
A low platelet count may be associated with certain infections, some medications, immune conditions, or other causes. Very low counts can be linked to easier bruising or bleeding, but interpretation requires a clinician.

## Why a value may be high
A high platelet count may be associated with inflammation, iron deficiency, recovery from blood loss, or other factors. A single value does not establish a cause.

## Questions for your doctor
You could ask what might explain the count, whether it should be repeated, and whether any related testing is appropriate.
